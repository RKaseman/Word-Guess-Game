I'm fascinated by the Hedonometer and have been looking for an opportunity to incorporate it into my coding ever since I stumbled across the API...that's no longer updated...ACK! Nevertheless, this game collects via scrape the 25 "happiest" scored and ranked words used by people on any given day.

That is a ridiculously broad way to define their work and its implications, but this is just my little demo hangman game. The Hedonometer is ironic in this setting and I like that.

Words don't repeat; 25 and the game is done. (I'll be revisiting it in the future to see if I can grab the entire word list for the selection set.)

Some of the words are variations of each other.

You can cheat and take a look at them but what fun is that?
